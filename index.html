Vocal Programming

Welcome to Vocal Programming. Vocal Programming is your personal code typist. All you have to do is speak out the code, and the app will write it for you!

Vocal Programming has certain syntax rules of it's own, and only responds to specific voice commands. Make sure you take a look at the document before you get started.
